ipShield

A framework for enforcing context-aware privacy on Android

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

ipShield gives you richer privacy control over sensor data on Android phones. Right now, many of the common sensors on your phone--along with the information they can yield--go unpoliced by the OS's data protection schemes. ipShield takes advantage of Android's virtualized execution environment to give you control over who can access your sensor information and how much of it they can see.
